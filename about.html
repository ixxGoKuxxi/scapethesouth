What it's made of

Our floors are constructed of sand, water, and mortar. For in-ground, We start by digging the ground space the floor is to fill. Once we have achieved the desired depth, we then find the appropriate slope for proper drainage of rain water. Now that we have completed those steps we start on laying the rocks. We, by default, keep mortar joints at 2 inches or less unless requested by client. Once floor has been set and dried we point-up (detail work) to achieve the finished product.

Sod Installs

~ Starting at ~
~ $1200.00 per 500 ft2 ~

What it's made of

We start by preping the ground. This includes tilling and clearing the ground (there is a $500.00 fee for removal of existing yard). Second, we smooth out the soft ground to remove any lumps.Then we add in some nice topsoil to ensure growth and health of your new sod. Once the topsoil is down, we rake that out flat. Now, it's time for the new grass! We use a staggerd pattern when installing sod to ensure that ground underneath does not wash away. All edges are cut in when necessary to keep your lawn looking sharp. Finally, we water in the new grass, roll it down, and clean up of the job site. Now kick back, take off your shoes and enjoy your new lawn.

Pictures and measurements are taken. We discuss with our clients their wants and needs required for plant success. A mock-up plan is drawn for the landscape under development.
